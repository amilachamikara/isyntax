Company is organized into departments.
A department has a department name, unique department number and a employee who is a manager.
Company records manager's ssn and start date.
Department may have several locations.
Each department has at least one employee who works in it.
Employee has name, birth date, address, sex and unique ssn.
Each employee has a supervisor.
Supervisor has a unique ssn.
The company keeps records of each employee's dependent.
They record dependent's name, sex, birth date and relationship to the employee.
Each department has a project. Each project has a project name, unique project number and a location.
Company keeps track of the current number of hours per week that an employee works on each project.
