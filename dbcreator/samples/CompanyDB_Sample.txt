Company is organized into departments.
A department has a department name, department number and a manager who manages it.
Company records manager's ssn and start date.
Department number has unique value for each department.
Department may have several locations.
Each department has employees who work in it.
Employee has name, birth date, address, sex, salary and he is uniquely identified by ssn.
Each employee has a direct supervisor.
Supervisor has a unique ssn.
The company keeps records of the dependents of each employee.
They record dependent's name, sex, birth date and relationship to the employee.
Each department is working number of projects and each project has a project name, unique project number and a location.
Company keep track of the current number of hours per week that an employee works on each project.
