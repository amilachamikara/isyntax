Each professor has a SSN, a name, an age, a rank, and a research specialty.
Each projects has a project number, a sponsor name, a starting date, an ending date, and a budget.
A graduate student has a SSN, a name, an age, and a degree program.
Each project is managed by one professor known as the project’s principal investigator.
Each project is worked on by one or more professors known as the project’s co-investigators.
Professors can manage and/or work on multiple projects.
Each project is managed by one or more research assistants who is a graduate student.
When graduate students work on a project, a professor must supervise their work on the project.
Graduate students can work on multiple projects. Graduate students will have a supervisor for each one.
A department has a department number, a department name, and a main office.
Each departments has a professor.
Professors work in one or more departments, and for each department that they work in, a time percentage is associated with their job.
Graduate students have one major department.
Each graduate student has a student advisor.
